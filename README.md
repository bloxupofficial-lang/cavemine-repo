# Mine A Cave | Roblox Game
Repository for Roblox front-page <ins>**Mine A Cave**</ins>.

### <ins> What is it about? </ins>

*Incremental Simulator*

* Mine in large procedurally-generated cave to look for valuable items.
* Fill up your backpack then come up to the surface to sell your goods.
* Buy better gear with the money you get.
* Repeat!

Mine in Cave for items, sell fore money, and use that money to get better items.

Minecarts can be used for faster mobility to get to the surface, they can also be used to empty your backpack.

The deeper you go, the better ores there are, but there are mobs that spawn to kill you.

There are over ***20*** different kinds of ores you can get!

## _Other features_
**Explosives** --> used for faster _cave_ _exploration_, also used to attack against mobs.

**Flags** --> You can potentially respawn here if you're killed instead of the surface.
